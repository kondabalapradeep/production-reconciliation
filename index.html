Method & rules baked into this dashboard
  • Finished goods: floor-reported quantity produced (all 5 category blocks per daily tab) vs Sage production entries (JR work-ticket receipts + BR BOM receipts). Gap After Adjustments adds net IA + IZ on the item — where a posting error was trued up by adjustment, the gap closes.
  • Raw materials: theoretical = floor production × BOM (multi-level explosion, no scrap %, material lines only); Actual = JI + BI issues. Deviation = Actual − Theoretical (Floor×BOM). Adjustments are applied to finished goods only.
  • Comparison window = overlap of the production dates and the transaction dates, computed automatically from the files.
  • Excluded item lines: D line, Plantation, Worth St (not item codes).
  • Costs: raw material cost = qty × LastTotalUnitCost from CI_Item. Finished-goods / batch unit cost = BOM roll-up (sum of all component quantities × their unit costs, through every BOM level). Deviation Cost = deviation qty × unit cost.
  • Known UOM items (A13, AA11, AX11) are flagged, not judged.
  • Nothing uploads to any server — files are read locally in the browser.
